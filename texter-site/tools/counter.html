What is Counter (Multi-Tool)?

All-in-one counter and formatter with checkboxes. This tool runs entirely in your browser and does not upload your text anywhere.

How to use this tool

  1. Paste or type your text into the large input box.
  2. Use the options above to customize processing.
  3. Results update in real time in the output panel.

FAQs

Is my text private? Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Does it work offline? If the page is open, yes; otherwise you need to load it once.

Can I share results? You can copy the output from the output panel.
